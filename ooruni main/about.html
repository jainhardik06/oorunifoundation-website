We are a nonprofit team working worldwide

Ooruni Foundation is a Non Profit and a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) which anchors, augments and advocates the pivotal 3Es of human life – Education, Environment and Equal Opportunities for a sustainable community development. Ooruni Foundation is a registered Trust, formed by six members during Chennai floods in 2015. Ooruni has got more than 100 dedicated volunteers and most of them are corporate employees.

Our Mission

Empowering communities through education, inclusion, and environmental sustainability.

Ooruni Foundation focuses on making education accessible under EduFuture India, supporting underprivileged children, especially through I Support a Girl for wards of HIV-positive parents. Environmental sustainability is prioritized through the Mass Plantation Drive, planting over 3600 saplings across Tamil Nadu. Initiatives like We Are Your Voice and Give Their World aim to empower differently-abled individuals by providing opportunities and supportive environments.

Our Vision

Creating an inclusive, sustainable, and empowered society.

Ooruni Foundation envisions equal opportunities for all, especially women, through programs like AWAKE for aspiring women entrepreneurs and the Working Women Achievers Award (WWAA), celebrating their achievements and resilience. The vision extends to fostering education, empowering women, and promoting a greener future for generations to come.

Our Goals

Affordable Education for All

Under EduFuture India, we make education accessible for underprivileged kids, especially through initiatives like I Support a Girl for children of HIV-positive parents.

Greener Earth

Through the Mass Plantation Drive, we planted over 3,600 saplings across Tamil Nadu by October 2019, promoting sustainability and a greener planet.

Empowering Differently-Abled

Initiatives like We Are Your Voice and Give Their World connect differently-abled students with opportunities and platforms to showcase their talents.

Women Empowerment

Through AWAKE (Advocacy to Women’s Ability and Knowledge for Empowerment), we support aspiring women entrepreneurs and provide equal opportunities for growth.

Recognizing Women Achievers

The Working Women Achievers Award (WWAA) highlights the achievements of women who overcome challenges and transform them into stepping stones for success.
