EduFutureIndia


Education is the basic right of every Indian citizen, a key to empower individuals and the only best way to a brighter tomorrow. Ooruni foundation has always been a great patron for the equal rights for education of all. EduFutureIndia is an originative effort by Ooruni Foundation to give an authenticated platform to all the prospective sponsors who would be interested in funding the education of needy students.

A transparent process by which the applications are invited, verified, and enrolled for utilizing the funds is in place. Regular monthly contributions are made by individuals or organizations who are willing to extend their support to Ooruni Foundation.

The potentialities should be developed with proper care and nourishment. The innate powers of the individuals should be properly cared for and given scope to develop. Our vision is to make this into reality.
